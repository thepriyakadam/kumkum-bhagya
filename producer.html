Kumkum Bhagya (English title: Twist of Fate) is an Indian drama television series produced by Ekta Kapoor's Balaji Telefilms. It premiered on 15 April 2014 on Zee TV. Starring Sriti Jha and Shabir Ahluwalia, it is loosely based on Sense and Sensibility by Jane Austen. ... Later, the show revolved around Abhi and Pragya.
